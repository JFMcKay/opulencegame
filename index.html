Objective: To collect the most matching pairs of fill-in-the-blank sentences and their corresponding answers, while incorporating the principles of the Law of Opulence.

Gameplay:

  1. Shuffle both sets of cards separately.
  2. Players draw one card from the fill-in-the-blank sentence deck and read it aloud.
  3. The other players then draw one card from the answer deck and try to match it with the sentence on the first player's card.
  4. The player who successfully matches the answer card with the sentence card collects the pair and keeps it face up in front of them.
  5. If no one is able to match the sentence card with an answer card, the player who drew the sentence card must draw a new answer card and try again on their next turn.
  6. Play continues in this way until all the pairs have been matched.
  7. The player with the most matching pairs at the end of the game wins.

By incorporating the principles of the Law of Opulence in the fill-in-the-blank sentences and their corresponding answers, players can learn about and practice the Law of Opulence while having fun playing the game.
